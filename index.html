Morgan makes work exploring human thoughts and personalities in both the group and individual levels through collage, painting, printmaking, and photography. She has exhibited work in the UVU Woodbury museum of art, The Snow College Gallery, and Student Galleries. Morgan is an AFA Graduate from Snow College.

Justin Cresswell is a Utah based sculptor. He graduated from Grand County High School in 2014 and graduated from Snow College in Ephraim in 2020. He is the son of a wandering cowboy, and has spent time in Texas, Colorado, Idaho, and Utah. He has shown work in the Snow College Gallery, The Annex Gallery, and the Moab Museum Gallery. He has had works displayed in the exhibitions Artists in Residence, Works in Progress, Stewardship, and Material Cognitive.
